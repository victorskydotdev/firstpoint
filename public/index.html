Our ethos

We are Heart-led

With our hearts, we reach and search out for the the underprivileged, especially women and young girls who need the most help. We have found these to be widows, abused women suffering from domestic violence, single mothers and young girls that have found themselves on the streets. In the last 2 years, we have reached over 500 women.

We are Mind-led

We believe that educating the mindset and advocating for the correct mind set is key to empowering the underprivileged, especially women and young children.
