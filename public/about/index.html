Who we are

We recognize that the underprivileged women and girls are disproportionately affected by poverty and discrimination and suffer abuse mentally and violations in the realization of their rights, entitlements and access to central resources. Our experience has shown us that when equipped with the proper resources, women and young girls have the power to transform their own lives and help their entire family, hence readdressing the cause of poverty and social injustice in the community.

our vision

A voice to every underprivileged, especially women and children.

our mission

Our mission is embedded in our mandate which is to empower underprivileged women and young girls through relevant education, skills development, innovative health care and focused livelihood programs.

Why we do what we do

Firstpoint Allianz Foundation, is the leading heart and mind charity for the underprivileged especially women and children. We are committed to giving every underprivileged woman and young girl a voice. We work in the deemed as indigent, unsuitable, and poor, helping women and young girls tackle the barriers they face and supporting them to create empowring livesfor themselves, their families and the community. We are registered and incorporated under the Nigeria Corporate Affairs Commission Abuja - Nigeria and Canada Not-for-Proﬁt Corporation Act; CN-1455515-5 and also with State Ministry Women Affiars and Poverty Alleviation.

Our team

Olasunkanmi Muftau Adedoyin was born on March 2nd, 1971 in a sprawling town called Offa in Kwara State, Nigeria

He attended St. Cyprian Primary school and Offa Grammar School respectively. Proceeded to pursue University education in the northern part of the country and graduated with Bachelor of Science in Management Studies at Usman Dan Fodio University, Sokoto and thereafter did his National Youth Service in Cross Rivers State.

Olasunkami is an experienced and iconic entrepreneur whom have worked with many Freight Forwarding Company's before he eventually established his own company named Access Freight Int'l Ltd in which he has been the Managing Director over the years.

Olasunkanmi believed so much in his country, Nigeria, with a keen passion for the underprivileged, this was the foundation of his philanthropical philosophy, that it is important for humanity to give back to the society.

Adedunni Sakirat Adedoyin

She is a Nigerian and a social entrepreneur. She co- founded Firstpoint Allianz Foundation as a non- governmental organization (NGO) that focuses on improving the lives of indigent women, widows, children’s education support, orphans, and vulnerable children.

Her passion for Humanitarian and community service has made her stand her out among others. “Finding myself among the needy made me realize they are just disadvantaged as some are very intelligent and smart and what they need is help and support”. A single person can’t carry the burden, so the idea came to try and pull together, comfortable and compassionate individuals to support, impact and touch lives, to make living better.

She has years of working experience with some private establishment before proceeding to co founding a Non-Governmental Organisation (Firstpoint Allianz Foundation)

Prof . Kazeem Rasheed Akhilomen is a Professor of Church History in the Department of Religious Management and Cultural Studies, Faculty of Arts in Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma.

He graduated from the then Bendel State University, Ekpoma in the Department of Religious Studies and Philosophy in 1988. As a promising academic, he was employed by the University as a Graduate Assistant in 1989 after his completion of the mandatory NYSC.

He thereafter enrolled for Post Graduate Studies and obtained M.A. Degree and Ph.D in Church History in 1993 and 1999 respectively.

Prof. Kazeem as he is fondly called by many has held so many academic and administrative positions within and outside the University; the most recent being the Dean, Students’ Affair Division, Ambrose Alli University (2016 - 2019).
