Who we are

We recognize that the underprivileged women and girls are disproportionately affected by poverty and discrimination and suffer abuse mentally and violations in the realization of their rights, entitlements and access to central resources. Our experience has shown us that when equipped with the proper resources, women and young girls have the power to transform their own lives and help their entire family, hence readdressing the cause of poverty and social injustice in the community.

our vision

A voice to every underprivileged, especially women and children.

our mission

Our mission is embedded in our mandate which is to empower underprivileged women and young girls through relevant education, skills development, innovative health care and focused livelihood programs.
